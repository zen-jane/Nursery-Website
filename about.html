ABOUT US

Bringing Nature Closer to Your Home Since 2010

WHO WE ARE ?

Green Nursery is your trusted partner for healthy plants and modern gardening. From indoor beauties to outdoor landscapes, we bring freshness and greenery to your space. Our mission is to make every home and office a little closer to nature.

With a team of passionate horticulturists and garden designers, we provide expert advice, quality plants, and professional landscaping services to transform your space into a green oasis.

Our Mission

To spread greenery and happiness by providing fresh, sustainable plants and professional gardening services.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan’s most trusted nursery brand, bringing nature closer to homes, offices, and communities.

Our Promise

Healthy plants, expert care, and customer satisfaction with every purchase.

OUR JOURNEY

2010

Started as a small nursery shop in Karachi with a vision to bring greenery to urban spaces.

2013

Introduced landscaping services and expanded our plant collection to over 50 varieties.

2015

Expanded with 100+ plant varieties and opened our second location in Lahore.

2018

Launched corporate services, bringing greenery to offices and commercial spaces.

2020

Launched online store and nationwide delivery during the pandemic to serve home gardeners.

2023

Opened our largest nursery yet in Islamabad, featuring a garden café and educational center.

2025

Now serving 10,000+ happy customers across Pakistan with 5 locations nationwide.
